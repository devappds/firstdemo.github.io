how it works

A “Boo-judicator” is a wallet that holds at least 100,000 $BOO tokens.

To vote, a Boo-judicator only needs to select which Destination they are voting for on the Nuclear Boo dApp, before the Nuclear wallet hits $20,000.

Each Boo-judicator is entitled to 1 vote per Nuclear Boo, plus any additional votes from holding Boo NFTs. Each Boo-judicator’s vote will be published on our dApp.

If a Boo-judicator fails to vote in the current Nuclear round, their vote(s) will not be stacked on the next vote, it will be reset. Once vote(s) are submitted, they cannot be withdrawn.
